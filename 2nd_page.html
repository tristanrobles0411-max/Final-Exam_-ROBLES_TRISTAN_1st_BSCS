Characters

104th Corps & Leadership

104th Training Corps & Main Trio



Eren Jaeger: Driven by a singular, burning hatred for Titans after witnessing his mother’s death. He is impulsive, idealistic, and possesses a mysterious ability that changes the course of the war.
Mikasa Ackerman: A prodigy with near-superhuman combat skills. She is fiercely protective of Eren, viewing him as her only remaining family.
Armin Arlert: Lacking in physical strength but possessing a genius-level intellect. He dreams of seeing the ocean beyond the walls and serves as the group’s primary strategist.
Annie Leonhart: A stoic, solitary fighter with an exceptional hand-to-hand combat style. She maintains a cold distance from her peers.
Reiner Braun: The "big brother" figure of the 104th. Strong-willed and dependable, he is highly respected by his fellow cadets.
Bertolt Hoover: Quiet and unassuming, he possesses great potential but lacks the initiative to lead. He is almost always seen at Reiner’s side.
Jean Kirschstein: An honest (sometimes brutally so) individual who initially wants a safe life in the Military Police. He shares a fierce rivalry with Eren.
Sasha Blouse: Known as "Potato Girl," she has incredible instincts and a compulsive appetite, often providing rare moments of levity.
Conny Springer: Outgoing and somewhat dim-witted, he is agile in combat and deeply loyal to his friends.
Marco Bodt: A natural leader with a kind heart who hopes to serve in the Military Police to help the King.
Ymir: Cynical, selfish, and deeply observant. She seems to care only about Krista, often mocking others for their idealism.
Krista Lenz / Historia Reiss: The "Goddess" of the 104th. She is incredibly kind and selfless, though she harbors a hidden past regarding her noble lineage.

Scout Regiment & Garrison



Erwin Smith: 13th Commander of the Scouts. A brilliant, cold, and calculated leader.
Levi Ackerman: Humanity's Strongest Soldier. Harsh personality but unrivaled skill.
Hange Zoë: Eccentric officer with an obsessive scientific interest in Titan biology.
Petra Rall: Kind-hearted elite soldier in Levi's squad.
Hannes: Garrison soldier and family friend. Carries deep guilt from the first attack.
Keith Shadis: Terrifying head instructor of the 104th.

Key Titans



Colossal Titan: 60-meter giant that ended a century of peace.
Armored Titan: Covered in plate-like skin armor. Breached the inner gate.
Female Titan: Intelligent Titan with hardening abilities and high-level combat skills.
Smiling Titan: The eerie Titan that killed Eren’s mother.

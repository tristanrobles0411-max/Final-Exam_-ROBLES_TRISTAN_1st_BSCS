Attack on Titan

The Journey Begins

The Beginning of the End



For a century, humanity lived in peace behind three massive circular walls Wall Maria, Wall Rose, and Wall Sina safe from the towering, man eating monsters known as Titans. Young Eren Jaeger, along with his adopted sister Mikasa Ackerman and their friend Armin Arlert, lived in the outermost district of Shiganshina. Eren longed to see the outside world, chafing against the cage humanity had accepted.

That peace was violently shattered when a 60 meter Colossal Titan appeared out of nowhere, kicking a hole in the outer gate. As Titans flooded the city, a terrifying Armored Titan smashed through the inner gate, forcing humanity to abandon Wall Maria. In the chaos, Eren watched helplessly as a Smiling Titan devoured his mother. On that day, he made a vow: to wipe every last Titan from the face of the earth.

Determined to fight, Eren, Mikasa, and Armin joined the military, surviving the grueling training of the 104th Cadet Corps. Five years later, just as they graduated, the Colossal Titan struck again at the Trost District. During the desperate battle, Eren was swallowed whole while saving Armin. However, within the belly of the beast, Eren's sheer will to live unlocked a hidden, terrifying power—he transformed into a Titan himself. Using this new power, humanity secured its first victory by sealing the breached gate.

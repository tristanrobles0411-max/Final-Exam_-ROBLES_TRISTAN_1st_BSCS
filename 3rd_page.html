Episode Guide

Attack on Titan Season 1

Season 1 Episodes



1. To You, in 2,000 Years - The Colossal Titan breaches the wall in Shiganshina.
2. That Day - Wall Maria falls as survivors flee to Wall Rose.
3. A Dim Light Amid Despair - Eren and others join the 104th Cadet Corps.
4. Night of the Disbanding - Cadets graduate just before a new attack begins.
5. First Battle - Eren is swallowed by a Titan while saving Armin.
6. The World the Girl Saw - Mikasa fights to survive in Trost District.
7. Small Blade - A mysterious Rogue Titan begins attacking other Titans.
8. I Can Hear a Heartbeat - Eren emerges from the Rogue Titan's body.
9. Whereabouts of His Left Arm - The military confronts Eren about his powers.
10. Response - Armin convinces the military that Eren is an ally.
11. Idol - Pixis devises a plan to retake Trost using Eren.
12. Wound - Eren struggles for control in his Titan form.
13. Primal Desire - The hole in Trost is sealed, marking a human victory.
14. Still Can't See - Eren faces a military tribunal to decide his fate.
15. Special Operations Squad - Eren joins Levi's elite squad for monitoring.
16. What Needs to be Done Now - The 104th graduates join their respective regiments.
17. Female Titan - A high-intelligence Female Titan attacks the Scouts.
18. Forest of Giant Trees - The Scouts lead the Titan into a trap in the forest.
19. Bite - The Scouts try to unmask the human inside the Female Titan.
20. Erwin Smith - The Titan escapes using an unexpected scream.
21. Iron Hammer - Levi's squad is wiped out by the Female Titan.
22. The Defeated - The Scouts retreat to the walls in failure.
23. Smile - Annie Leonhart is revealed to be the Female Titan.
24. Mercy - A brutal battle erupts inside Stohess District.
25. Wall - Annie encases herself in crystal as a secret is revealed.
